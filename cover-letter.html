Build the intelligence layer that earns machine authority.

Dear 4MP and Xora hiring team,

4MP is attempting something much harder than applying AI to machining data. You are building the system that must interpret manufacturing intent, understand imperfect physical state, explain deviation, generate a correction, prove that correction, and decide what knowledge can safely become reusable. That is the mandate that interests me.

My career has repeatedly lived at the interfaces this company must make coherent. At Vape-Jet, I connect software-enabled robots, machine vision, telemetry, factory operations, quality, engineering, field service, customers, and approximately 1,000 deployed machines into an operating system that detects risk earlier and closes work more reliably. Through DudeWorth, I design agentic workflows with structured context, evaluations, human authority, escalation, governance, and decision records. Earlier roles brought high-reliability electronics, manufacturing, root cause, autonomous drone operations, computer vision, and Amazon-scale operating mechanisms.

I would approach 4MP's AI platform through a governed correction object: manufacturing intent, reconstructed state, residual evidence, cause hypothesis, confidence, correction locus, authority boundary, validation plan, post-action evidence, and learning scope. The model is not the product by itself. The product is a correction system that fails legibly before it fails physically.

The strongest first move is not to design the entire platform in abstraction. It is to select one valuable correction class, close it end to end in shadow mode, stress the boundary across machine, tool, material, program, environment, and operator variation, then use that evidence to install the broader architecture and team.

I created an independent candidate vision at russelldudek.github.io/Xora/. It includes the Autonomous Correction Kernel, a 120-day entry plan, an interview thesis brief, and a Correction Qualification Record. These are candidate models, not claims about undisclosed 4MP systems.

I would welcome a technical working session around one representative correction problem. The goal would be simple: make my architecture judgment visible and determine whether my cross-domain physical-AI leadership can accelerate the company 4MP is becoming.

Carpe diem,
Russell Dudek
