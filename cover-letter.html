Re: Vice President of Artificial Intelligence

4MP is tackling a problem that becomes more demanding precisely when the product becomes more valuable: digital manufacturing intent may be exact, but the physical machine, tool, fixture, environment, and material state are not. The leader must therefore do more than build models. The role must create the decision architecture that explains residuals, bounds intervention, proves results, and determines what learning may responsibly generalize.

That integration point is where my career has repeatedly operated. At Vape-Jet, I connect factory, field, customer, quality, support, engineering, ERP, telemetry, and approximately 1,000 fielded robots into earlier risk detection, prioritized work, standard work, and accountability. Through DudeWorth, I design agentic workflows that combine structured context, retrieval, human judgment, escalation, evaluation, and decision records. At Compunetics, I led engineering, manufacturing, quality, customer delivery, root cause, and process controls in advanced electronics and high-reliability environments.

My experience is adjacent—not a substitute for—the deepest current CNC-controls and production-ML specialization in the specification. The value I bring is a complementary systems authority: the ability to connect machine evidence, technical teams, operating reality, quality discipline, human decision rights, and adoption into one repeatable mechanism. My autonomous-systems work adds disciplined safety and consequence awareness; my Amazon experience adds launch and operating mechanisms at scale.

I built an independent candidate vision at https://russelldudek.github.io/Xora/ to make that reasoning inspectable. Its Precision Workpiece shows how residual, authority, proof, and learning scope change across thermal drift, tool wear, fixture shift, and program ambiguity. The companion 120-day plan and Correction Qualification Record show how I would begin with one instrumented correction class, establish a replayable evidence contract, stress the boundary, and only then install the compounding platform and organization.

I would welcome a working discussion around one representative correction problem: reconstruct the intent and physical state, separate measured facts from cause hypotheses, define the AI/physics/rules boundaries, define authority and rollback, specify the minimum proof, and decide what may become reusable knowledge. That session would make visible both the opportunity and the limits of my contribution.

Carpe diem,
