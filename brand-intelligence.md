- 4MP official site and public product material used for company and product recognition.
- Xora Innovation official site and portfolio/company-formation material used for investor context.

## Color token provenance

- `--brand-blue: #075eff` - source-sampled from the public 4MP logo and digital presence.
- `--brand-ink`, `--brand-paper`, `--brand-fog`, `--brand-steel`, and `--brand-rule` - source-derived neutral system matching 4MP's precise technical presentation.
- `--inspection-cyan`, `--inspection-amber`, `--inspection-coral`, and `--inspection-green` - candidate-original semantic accents used only for measurement, uncertainty, hold, and qualified states.

## Typography evidence and decision

4MP's public presentation uses tightly controlled geometric/industrial sans-serif hierarchy with compact technical labeling. Proprietary font licensing was not established. The campaign therefore uses installed, broadly available substitutes:

- Noto Sans Display for condensed technical display hierarchy.
- Lato for humanist body copy and executive documents.
- Noto Sans Mono for coordinates, state labels, and measurement notation.

No proprietary font files are copied or committed.

## Photography and interface character

- Product-led, precise, clean, technical, high-key industrial imagery.
- Measured geometry, datum references, coordinate notation, contour lines, section cuts, and inspection overlays.
- Motion is calm and causal: measure, isolate, constrain, correct, verify, settle.

## Visible-use decision

- Website hero: official 4MP logo and public workpiece image, surrounded by candidate-original measurement and qualification overlays.
- Resume and cover letter: restrained 4MP blue, technical rules, datum labels, and a small company identifier; no fake corporate letterhead.
- Brief, entry plan, and qualification record: shared precision-rule grammar, tabular figures, annotation labels, and page furniture.

## Independent-candidate distinction

Every public surface states that it is an independent candidate work product. 4MP and Xora have not reviewed or endorsed it. The visual system communicates Russell's point of view without imitating an official company site or internal product interface.
