## Typography
The official site uses a modern high-contrast sans-serif/condensed display character. No proprietary font files are copied. The campaign uses licensed/system substitutes through Aptos Display, Arial Narrow, and broadly available sans-serif/monospace fallbacks, reproducing the official character through strong scale, condensed display hierarchy, and precise technical labels.

## Product and motion cues
The public 4MP story emphasizes CNC utilization, ideal digital intent versus imperfect physical reality, in-process metrology, intelligent correction, first-pass quality, distributed manufacturing, and keeping machines cutting rather than waiting. The campaign translates those cues into contour comparison, qualification boundaries, evidence gates, and one-shot correction-state motion.
