AUTONOMOUS CORRECTION KERNEL

Six states. One accountable correction.

Each transition names the evidence required to proceed and the condition that forces a hold.

  1. Parse intentG-code, CAM context, geometry, tolerance, operator meaning.
  2. Reconstruct stateMachine, tool, fixture, material, environment, telemetry.
  3. Explain residualSeparate measured facts, hypotheses, confidence, uncertainty.
  4. Bound actionCorrection locus, authority, limit, rollback, hold condition.
  5. Prove resultReplay, simulation, shadow inference, measurement.
  6. Admit to memoryRetain learning only with scope, version, evidence.
