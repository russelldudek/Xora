INTERVIEW THESIS BRIEF · PAGE 1

Executive thesis

Make every correction explainable, bounded, validated, and reusable.

The VP of AI mandate is to convert manufacturing intent, physical state, metrology, physics constraints, and production feedback into safe corrective action—and build the organization that makes the system compound.

COMPANY MOMENT

Company formation around autonomous precision

4MP publicly frames a gap between perfect digital intent and imperfect physical execution. Xora's company-formation and long-horizon investment posture increases the need for a durable technical and operating architecture.

ACTUAL MANDATE

Build a decision architecture, not only a model portfolio

Machine authority requires evidence, limits, ownership, fallback, qualification, and a controlled path from local correction to reusable learning.

Central tensions

Speed vs consequence

Move quickly without making uncertainty invisible.

Physics vs production variability

Use structure without pretending the shop floor is clean.

Local correction vs fleet learning

Generalize only with explicit context and scope.

Hands-on authority vs organization building

Recruit while staying credible in evidence and failure analysis.

What changes by scenario

Thermal drift

Coordinate/compensation model; shadow then bounded correction; proof across warm-up states.

Tool wear

Offset/life model; auto-adjust inside certified limits; trend plus post-correction probe.

Fixture shift

Work-coordinate transform or hold; human confirmation above the shift boundary.

Program ambiguity

G-code/CAM/operator intent; human clarification; simulation and safe dry run.

Qualification object

A correction record connects observed residual, inferred cause, evidence, permitted action, authority, validation result, reuse boundary, and learning-admission decision. It makes the correction explainable after the model has moved on.

Entry and executive discussion

DAYS 0—30

Instrument the problem

Choose one correction class, map intent and coordinate frames, establish lineage, and sign the authority and hold contract.

DAYS 31—60

Close a narrow loop

Build replay, shadow inference, human review, correction proposal, post-action measurement, and rollback.

DAYS 61—90

Stress the boundary

Test machine state, tool, material, programming style, operator, observability, and failure modes.

DAYS 91—120

Install the compounding system

Translate proof into team design, data architecture, ownership, simulation roadmap, and correction-class portfolio.

Executive questions
  1. Which correction class has the clearest economic value and the cleanest measurement loop?
  2. Where does 4MP currently want AI to recommend, require approval, or act inside certified limits?
  3. What state, coordinate-frame, and lineage assumptions create the greatest risk today?
  4. Which learning should remain machine-local, and what evidence would permit broader reuse?
  5. What must the first AI team make demonstrably true in twelve months?
